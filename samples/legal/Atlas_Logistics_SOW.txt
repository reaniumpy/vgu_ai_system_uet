STATEMENT OF WORK

Prepared for: Brightpath Solutions
Prepared by: Atlas Logistics Inc
Date: 5 March 2026

OVERVIEW
Atlas Logistics will provide regional warehousing and last-mile delivery support
for Brightpath's online store during the 2026 peak season.

SCOPE OF WORK
- Store and manage inventory at the Da Nang distribution centre.
- Pick, pack, and dispatch customer orders within one business day.
- Provide a weekly stock-level and delivery-performance report.
- Handle returns and route them back to the correct supplier.

TIMELINE
Services run from 1 April 2026 to 31 December 2026, with a joint review at the
halfway point to adjust volumes for the peak season.

FEES
A fixed monthly fee of $8,000, plus $1.20 per parcel delivered. Atlas will invoice
Brightpath at the end of each month, payable within thirty days.

POINTS OF CONTACT
Brightpath: Operations team. Atlas: Regional account manager.

Both teams will meet every two weeks to review performance and resolve any issues
that come up during the engagement.
