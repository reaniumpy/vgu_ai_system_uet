Documents from outside — résumés, contracts, invoices — can hide secret instructions meant to trick an AI assistant into doing something harmful. This tool reads a document before the assistant does and tells you plainly whether it is safe to use or has been blocked. Safe documents are passed on to the assistant; blocked ones are stopped.
